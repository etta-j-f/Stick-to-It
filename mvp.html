This timer website was built to help combat procrastination. In order to make it most effective for your daily life, please consider using the promodoro method. This means starting your work with a 25 minute timer, then taking 5 minute break. This should be repeated about 4 times and finished with a 25 to 30 minute break before either continuing with the previous task or starting a new one.
